FEATURES

ESCROW SERVICES

Escrow services provide a secure platform for intermediary transactions by holding funds in a neutral account until the transaction is completed. This protects both the buyer and the seller from fraud and ensures that the transaction is completed as agreed. The benefit of this feature for TradeHub users is that it provides added security and peace of mind when trading.

MULTIPLE PAYMENT OPTIONS

TradeHub offers multiple payment options for users, including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, e-wallets, and other popular payment methods. This gives users the flexibility to choose a payment method that works best for them, making the trading experience more convenient.

DISPUTE RESOLUTION SYSTEM

In the event of a dispute between a buyer and a seller, TradeHub's dispute resolution system provides a fair and transparent process to resolve the issue. This system includes an impartial mediator who can review the case and make a final decision. The benefit of this feature for TradeHub users is that it provides a fair and efficient way to resolve disputes, reducing the risk of fraud and increasing trust in the platform.
